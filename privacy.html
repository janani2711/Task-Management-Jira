This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our Jira Visualization App (the "App"). This App is used internally within our company to visualize and analyze project data from Jira for reporting and management purposes.

Information We Collect

The App accesses limited project-related data from your Atlassian Jira account, including issue summaries, project details, user assignments, and workflow statuses. This information is retrieved solely for the purpose of creating visual dashboards and insights for company projects.

How We Use Information

  • To generate real-time visual reports and dashboards from Jira data.
  • To support project tracking, productivity analysis, and resource planning.
  • To maintain and improve the performance and functionality of the App.
  • To provide technical support if required.

Data Storage and Security

The App does not permanently store or share sensitive user data. All Jira data retrieved through the Atlassian API is processed in real-time and deleted after visualization or upon session termination. Access tokens are securely managed according to OAuth 2.0 best practices.
